## Research Snapshot

My work focuses on trustworthy AI & privacy-preserving systems. I study end-to-end pipelines—from adversarial threat surfaces to robust deployment—to make speech and multimodal models dependable in high-stakes environments. I enjoy collaborating across academia and industry to translate research findings into production-ready defenses.

I'm actively exploring new opportunities as a Software Engineer, Machine Learning Engineer, or Applied Scientist within the U.S. market.
